Intel module

Competitive Intelligence

Stop manually checking competitors. This module tracks what they post and turns it into simple reporting.

Start with Intake Back to Modules How it works

What Intel does

Monitor

Track competitor accounts and content activity over time.

Log

Write structured data into a sheet (posts, links, frequency, notes).

Summarize

Weekly summaries that tell you what changed and what to copy or avoid.

What to include in your request

  • Which competitors to track (names or URLs)
  • Which platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, X, LinkedIn)
  • Date range and reporting frequency (daily, weekly)
  • Where you want results stored (Google Sheets recommended)

Good example requests

  • “Track these 5 Instagram accounts weekly and log how many times they posted.”
  • “Every Sunday, generate a competitor summary with links and key topics.”
  • “Track brand posts from Sunday to Sunday and write the totals to a sheet.”
